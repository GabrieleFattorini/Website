About Me.

Welcome to my personal website!

My name is Gabriele Fattorini, I am 26 years old and I have a great passion for everything related to the web. In particular, I'm specialized in the creation and development of websites, starting from the sketch phase up to the creation of custom graphics and code development.

I started studying at the Nemo Academy in Florence, where I discovered this fantastic world. After learning the basics, I decided to continue with the Start2Impact path, to be able to specialize as a full stack developer.

I invite you to visit my portfolio, where you will find some of my projects. If you like them and want to make one with me, do not hesitate to contact me through the contact form.
