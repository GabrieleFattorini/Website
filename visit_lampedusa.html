Visit Lampedusa.

This is a project I made for a client from Lampedusa (Italy). Specifically, it's a website for a tourist agency of the island.

The website opens with a "hero section", where you are immediately redirected to the main services of "Visit Lampedusa". Within the website, you can order specific island activities, view photos taken directly by tourists and view their reviews. You can also register as a business and collaborate with the agency itself, offering your service.

If you need assistance, you can contact them through a specific form. If you want to do a specific search within the website, you can do it through the search field at the top right of the menu.

As for the design of the website, as you can see it is simple and minimal.

The color palette consists of three main colors: white prevails, with shades of purple and green for the titles and calls to action.

The corners of the buttons and cards are rounded, so as to give more lightness to the whole. As for the cards, this is exactly how the various activities are displayed (a slight shadow is shown in hover). Same thing for the login page, for the reviews and for the other elements that make up the website.

The website is fully responsive, as you can see from the two mockups below.
